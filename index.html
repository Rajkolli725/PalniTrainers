Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our training, consulting and staffing services.

What does VTech Infotech do?

VTech Infotech is a full-spectrum IT services company offering expert-led training, IT consulting, and staffing across ServiceNow, Salesforce, Cloud, Data and AI.

Who are your courses for?

Our programs suit beginners looking to break into tech, working professionals upskilling for new roles, and teams seeking corporate training. Any pre-requisites are listed on each course page.

Are classes live or self-paced?

We offer live, instructor-led sessions with hands-on labs, plus recordings and materials for self-paced review. Weekday, weekend and fast-track cohorts are available.

Do you provide certificates and certification preparation?

Yes. You receive a course completion certificate and targeted guidance, mock exams and resources to help you clear the official certifications listed on each course.

Do you help with job placement?

We provide resume reviews, interview preparation and career guidance, and we place vetted talent with our hiring partners for contract, contract-to-hire and full-time roles.

How do I enroll or book a free demo?

Click “Enroll Now” or “Book Free Demo” on any course, or “Talk to an Advisor” anywhere on the site. A course advisor will reach out within one business day.

Do you offer corporate or team training?

Yes. We design custom private cohorts tailored to your team’s goals, schedule and tech stack. Use “Talk to an Advisor” to start a conversation.

How can I contact support?

Email support@vtechinfotech.com or call +1 (214) 501-0690. See the Contact section on our home page for hours.
